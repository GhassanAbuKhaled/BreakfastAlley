FRESH FROM THE START

NEW MENU QUARTERLY

We started as neighbors who loved to meet up to start the day right.

One was a baker who made the best sourdough on the planet. Another was a casual farmer, whose greens always made us grin. Finally, the breakfast host was a home chef who loved to create what she called "Breakfast Breakthroughs".

We shared these humble breakfasts in the alley that connected our homes. We decided to share this lovely day-starter with the greater community. Breakfast Alley was born - our greatest breakthrough yet!

Frequently asked questions

CAN I ORDER TAKEOUT OR SIMPLY PICK UP THE FOOD?

Of course! We're a tiny restaurant, so takeout is a way for us to serve more people the good food they deserve. Just call 123-456-7890 for advance orders.

HOW OFTEN DO YOU CHANGE THE MENU?

We typically change every quarter, to coincide with the changes in seasonal produce. We make everything fresh, so this is crucial for us. However, our Sumptuous Staples menu stays the same all year.
